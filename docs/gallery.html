Design: A Hair Clipper

In early 2019, I designed a simple hair clipper that looks like a nail clipper but is for cutting hair bangs. The goal is to allow individuals to cut their hair bangs neatly and easily without minimal technical requirements. I tested it on a cosmetology head and, actually, my own hair.
