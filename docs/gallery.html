Design: A Hair Clipper

In early 2019, I designed a simple hair clipper that looks like a nail clipper, but is used for hair bangs. The goal is to allow individuals to cut their hair bangs neatly with ease and no minimal technical required. I tested it on a cosmetology head and actually my own hair.
