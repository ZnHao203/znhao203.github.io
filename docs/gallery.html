City of Fantasy: D&D Modelling

I've always loved playing D&D games with my friends, crafting our own stories and adventures. One of the by-products of these storiesis this model of an imaginary city, created in 2021. The striking yellow building you see is designed to be a small museum.
I have a deep affection for this design, and one of my key milestones in 3D modelling is to expand this city and add even more intricate details!

Design: A Hair Clipper

In early 2019, I designed a small hair clipper as the final project for my Design Technology course. It resembles a nail clipper but is intended for trimming hair bangs. The coal was to enable individuals to cut their bangs neatly and easioly with minimal technical skill required.
I tested the clipper on both a cosmetology head and my own hair.
Later, I filed a patent application, which was approved in early 2020. Patent Link (in Chinese).
